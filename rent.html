CUNY Rental Services

We rent out a wide range of technology items. While most items have short loan periods, selected items (such as Chromebooks and graphing calculators) can be checked out for the entire semester. All you need is your CUNYme app to scan for rentals.

  • Week long (7 days)

Additional Information

Macbooks

What's Included:

  • Microsoft Office 2016 for Mac (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
  • Browsers (Chrome, Firefox, and Safari)
  • Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign)
  • A MacBook Pro with assistive technology–Kurzweil 3000 and Dragon Pro–is also available

Windows Laptops

What’s Included:

  • Microsoft Office 2016 (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Access)
  • Browsers (Chrome, Firefox, and Internet Explorer)
  • Zoom
  • A Dell Latitude 5580 with assistive technology is also available.

Tablets

What's Included:

  • 32 GB Storage
  • Devices are factory reset before they are loaned so students can add any apps
  • Tablets with assistive technology also available

Chargers

What's Included:

  • Power plug and cable
  • Phone and laptop chargers available
  • Micro-USB, USB-C, and lightning cables available

Cameras

What's Included:

  • 16mm-50mm lens
  • USB charging cable and AC adapter

Headphones

What's Included:

  • USB headphones (basic)

Calculators

What's Included:

  • Graphing calculators, Basic calculators, Financial Calculators, Scientific Calculators available

Hearing Assistive Technology

What's Included:

  • Roger MyLink with headphones (instructions for use)
  • Roger Pen (instructions for use)
  • Roger Table Mic (instructions for use)

Book Rentals

From Other Libraries:

  • Borrow from the New York Public Library, Brooklyn Public Library, or Queens Library

  • Borrow from Other Libraries
    • Not available at CUNY? Get books and articles delivered from other libraries in 3-10 days
